Tube Service is an app for London Underground commuters. It gives you the status of all London Underground lines as well as live tube arrivals at your favourite stations.

Tube Service features:

- See the current status of all London Underground lines (including DLR, Trams and the Elizabeth line).
- Tap to see a more detailed status for each line.
- Search and view the live arrivals boards for a particular tube station.
- Save your favourite arrivals boards for easy access in future.
- Find stations nearby (including directions on how to get there)
- Plan your journeys across London with the Journey Planner
- Quick links to train maps
- View the latest TfL posts on X (formerly Twitter)
